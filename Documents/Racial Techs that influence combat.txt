== The Federation of Sol

Mark II Advanced Carriers (4)  Carriers may carry eight units and gain sustain damage.

Spec Ops Training (5)  Each time you roll a 1 during Invasion Combat, you may reroll the die. You must use the second result.

== The Sardakk N’orr

At the end of a round of Invasion Combat, roll one die for each casualty you take. For each 10 you roll, your opponent must immediately take one casualty.

== The Brotherhood of Yin

Use your racial ability twice each ground combat.
